Heart Surgery is one of the most advanced and life-saving procedures in modern medicine. It is performed to treat a wide range of heart conditions including coronary artery disease, heart valve disorders, congenital heart defects, and heart failure. With the help of highly trained cardiac surgeons and cutting-edge technology, heart surgery can restore normal blood flow, improve heart function, and significantly enhance the quality of life.

Whether it's an open-heart surgery, minimally invasive procedure, or a heart transplant, each operation requires careful planning, precision, and post-operative care.
